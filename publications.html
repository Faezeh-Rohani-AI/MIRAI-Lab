Extracting Gait and Balance Pattern Features from Skeleton Data to Diagnose Attention Deficit/Hyperactivity Disorder in Children

Faezeh Rohani

The Journal of Supercomputing · Springer Nature · Published 2024

Topic: ADHD Diagnosis, Gait Analysis, Balance Assessment, Skeleton Data, Motor Behavior, Pediatric Mental Health.

This paper presents a method for extracting gait and balance pattern features from skeleton data to support the diagnosis of Attention Deficit/Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD) in children, contributing to AI-driven diagnostic tools for pediatric mental health.
